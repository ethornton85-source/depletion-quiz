# Quiz Spec — "How Depleted Are You Really?"

## Scoring model
- 7 questions, each answer scored 0 / 1 / 2 points.
- Max total = 14.
- Tier mapping:
  - **Tier 1 — Situationally Depleted:** 0–5
  - **Tier 2 — Chronically Running on Empty:** 6–10
  - **Tier 3 — Hitting a Wall:** 11–14

## State tags (drive personalized ritual tools)
Two key questions tag the user's nervous-system "flavor":
- Q2 (end of workday) → `wired` | `flat` | `drained`
- Q4 (energy through day) → `wired` | `flat` | `drained`

Aggregate dominant tag → pick ritual tools:
- **wired/tense** → calming/vagal: EMDR audio, narrowing-vision technique, vagus steering-wheel pressure, dive reflex
- **flat/checked-out** → energizing: peppermint scent/candy, EMDR audio, 2-min intention
- **drained** → blend of grounding + gentle activation: dive reflex, 2-min intention, peppermint

## Questions & options (value: points | tag)
Q1. How do you feel most mornings when you wake up?
- Refreshed (0)
- Tired but functional (1)
- Exhausted before the day starts (2)

Q2. By the time you get home from work, you feel:
- Wired and tense (1 | wired)
- Flat and checked out (1 | flat)
- Completely drained — like nothing is left (2 | drained)
- Actually okay, mostly (0 | flat)

Q3. How often do you snap at your kids or partner in the evenings?
- Rarely (0)
- A few times a week (1)
- Almost every day (2)

Q4. How would you describe your energy throughout the day?
- Mostly steady (0)
- Peaks and crashes (1 | flat)
- Running on caffeine or stress just to get through (2 | wired)

Q5. How do you feel after a full night of sleep?
- Restored (0)
- Somewhat better but still tired (1)
- Sleep doesn't seem to help much (2)

Q6. When you try to be present with your family, it feels:
- Natural most of the time (0)
- Like I have to force it (1)
- Impossible — I'm physically there but mentally gone (2 | drained)

Q7. How long have you felt this way?
- A few weeks (0)
- Several months (1)
- I honestly can't remember feeling any other way (2)
